Indie app santa

What is it?

Indie app santa is like an advent calendar but for apps made by indie iOS developers, from December 1st to 24th they share their apps mostly for free but sometimes it can be a discount for in app purchase.

You can follow the official Twitter account for updates or download the official app or... visit the official site!
You can even set up a widget via Scriptable app! Here's the code.

Note that this repository has nothing to do with Indie app santa, it's made by me just for fun and for archive.

Have an awesome christmas
